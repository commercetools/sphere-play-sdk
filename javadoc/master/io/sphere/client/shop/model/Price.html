io.sphere.client.shop.model

Class Price

  • java.lang.Object
    • io.sphere.client.shop.model.Price

  • public class Price
extends java.lang.Object
    Price of a product variant. A price has a value and can be optionally restricted to a specific country and customer group.

    Examples

    • (10EUR, DE, gold) - applies to gold customers in Germany
    • (15EUR, DE) - applies to customers in Germany
    • (20EUR) - applies to all customers
